THE BATTLE FOR RADIANCES TOTALUS IV

31st August 2025  |  7th City, Sandiacre  |  Mid Heresy

Following the Schism of Mars, in which the Taghmata Mortivar played only a limited role, Mortivar and supporting Mechanicum forces were dispatched to the very cusp of the Eye of Terror to take part in the campaign for Radiances Totalus IV.

BATTLE 01

Reclamation of Dread March Eternal

1500 PTS vs. Ultramarines & Dark Angels DECISIVE VICTORY

Objective: Acquire and secure the remains of a fallen Imperator Titan of the Legio Mortis

With Clan Ayreas of the Iron Hands providing tactically vital subterranean support, Mortivar enacted the Doctrine of Searing Silence — a systematic voiding of the irradiated manufactorum flats that surrounded the half-buried wreck of the Mortis Imperator-Class Titan Dread March Eternal. The Ultramarines' disciplined advance and the Dark Angels' stoic counter-strikes were caught off-sequence by Mortivar's aggressive deployment of Errax constructs and devastating lightning-cannon volleys of Krios battle tanks.

The loyalists were forced into tactical withdrawal, leaving behind dozens of disabled engines. Within a single solar hour, the Imperator's husk was fully secured under Mortivar's sigil, the first vault-breach complete, and multiple synaptic relics extracted.

BATTLE 02

The Ruins of Byzant Aeturnis

2000 PTS vs. White Scars & Salamanders MINOR DEFEAT

Objective: Seize research-grade vault-complexes hidden beneath shattered megalopolis

The ruined city that once bore the splendid name Byzant Aeturnis was a labyrinth of melted ferrocrete and collapsed hab blocks. Mortivar sought experimental exo-planar encryption cores believed to be stored in subterranean cogitator vaults.

However, the White Scars' lightning-fast encirclement tactics, combined with the Salamanders' tenacious siege methods, prevented the Taghmata from maintaining ground long enough to execute deep vault penetration. Clan Ayreas subterranean assets were forced to withdraw as orbital augury detected encroaching firestorm bombardment. The data-treasures remained buried beneath the rubble.

Mortivar recorded the loss as "an unacceptable but not terminal inefficiency."

BATTLE 03

The Betrayal of Ghul-Kharas Arcology

3000 PTS vs. World Eaters & House Mordred STALEMATE — STRATEGIC SUCCESS

Objective: Extraction and evacuation of secured relic-tech, denial of planet to remaining enemies

When Mortivar returned to the traitor stronghold of Ghul-Kharas, reinforced by a Warbringer Titan of Legio Mortis, the enemy were not loyalists but fellow traitors — blood-drunk World Eaters supported by the Knightly House Mordred, their heraldry as fractured as their sanity.

The neo-gothic arcology spire of Ghul-Kharas became a slaughterhouse of echoing vox-screams and ruptured reactor conduits. Mortivar had planned for an organized mechanized extraction, but the World Eaters' psychotic betrayal spiralled into uncontrollable carnage. Mechanicum constructs and chain-axe berserkers tore through one another in the streets, allowing the core of Mortivar's forces to escape the collapsing warzone while their enemies annihilated themselves in catastrophic cross-violence.

// CAMPAIGN OUTCOME //
1 DECISIVE VICTORY
1 MINOR DEFEAT
1 STRATEGIC SUCCESS

Radiances Totalus IV was subsequently reclaimed by the Imperium, but Mortivar deemed his cohort's actions on the world a success — primarily the Reclamation of Dread March Eternal, which improved his standing among the traitor Mechanicum and Legio Mortis.
