OPERATIONAL TIMELINE // TAGHMATA MORTIVAR

Chronological record of Archmagos Mortivar's actions before and during the Horus Heresy. Compiled from cogitator-vault archives and surviving field records.

ca. 003.M31
PRE-HERESY  |  VHALOR CORRIDOR

THE IVth LEGION RAIDS THE WAYSTATION ARCHIVES

In the years before the Heresy breaks, IVth Legion operatives move systematically through Mechanicum waystation-archives along the Vhalor warp corridor. Among the intelligence taken from Mortivar's secured research caches: fragmented but recoverable copies of his consciousness-replication lattice — the core of a decades-long programme that would, in his own words, render death an administrative problem.

The Iron Warriors do not know what they have stolen. To them, it is leverage, or merely thorough. To Mortivar, it is an open wound in the data-record of the self. He marks it and waits.

005.M31
EARLY HERESY  |  MARS

THE SCHISM OF MARS — DEFENCE OF THE MORTIVAR HOLDINGS

When Fabricator-General Kelbor-Hal declares for Horus and civil war erupts across the Red Planet, Mortivar's forge-holdings become a priority target for the loyalist counter-offensive.

The Saturnyne Rams — a Solar Auxilia cohort operating under Rogal Dorn's broader effort to retrieve vital arms and armour from Mars — advance on his positions with Legio Ignatum engine support. The Fire Wasps' Titans press through the industrial wastelands surrounding Mortivar's outer cordon.

The Rams calculated we were a secondary objective.
That miscalculation cost them dearly.

ca. 006.M31
EARLY HERESY  |  HIVE KARTHAX, VHALOR XXVI

VHALOR XXVI — THE IRON RECKONING

An Iron Warriors grand cruiser, the Iron Reckoning, falls from the sky and embeds itself in the single hive of the minor world Vhalor XXVI. To the wider war, it represents fleet-routing archives and Legion deployment logs — intelligence worth fighting over. To Mortivar, it represents something far more specific: the stolen data, now drifting within reach.

The Autokratorii Regiment and Legio Cybernetica Cohort advance on Hive Karthax in support of the wider traitor assault. House Vextrix holds the vanguard. A Warhound of Legio Mortis marches with a cortex-monitoring relay of Mortivar's own design fitted to its manifold. The walls must be secured before the vaults can be opened.

ca. 009.M31
MID HERESY  |  EYE OF TERROR FRINGE

THE CAMPAIGN FOR RADIANCES TOTALUS IV

With the Schism of Mars concluded and his holdings on the Red Planet secured, Mortivar and supporting Mechanicum forces are dispatched to the very cusp of the Eye of Terror to take part in the campaign for Radiances Totalus IV, a world ancient enough to predate Imperial cartography.

Three engagements follow: the reclamation of the fallen Imperator Titan Dread March Eternal, the contested vault-complexes beneath the shattered city of Byzant Aeturnis, and the catastrophic betrayal at Ghul-Kharas Arcology, where the World Eaters turn on their own allies. Mortivar deems the campaign a net success. The Dread March Eternal reclamation improves his standing among the Legio Mortis. Other debts are noted for later settlement.

014.M31
LATE HERESY  |  TERRA

THE SIEGE OF TERRA

The campaign reaches its terminus. Mortivar and his Taghmata march with the host of Horus to the walls of the Imperial Palace — the final accounting of the age of flesh. Whether he is there to witness the end of the Emperor, to secure the data-vaults beneath the Eternity Gate, or simply because the calculus of self-preservation has carried him this far, only Mortivar's cogitator-records know.

He arrives. He endures. In some form or another, he always does.

The Siege is simply a very large discontinuity in the data-flow.
We have survived smaller ones.
